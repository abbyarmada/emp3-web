Dark Theme

Themes let you apply a consistent tone to an app. The theme specifies the darkness of the surfaces, level of shadow, and appropriate opacity of ink elements. To promote greater consistency between apps, light and dark themes are available to choose from.

For COE3 application development the Material Design Dark Theme is the target. Dark themes increase battery life and are more suited to tactical environments for decreased light emmisions and usability in low light scenarios. The various shades of background colors and their uses are illustrated below.

For web applications, the dark theme is already included in the minified COE3 CSS file ( coe3.min.css) that is used for this guide. For Smart Client applications the theme will be a setting in the application configuration.

1. Status bar
2. App bar
3. Background
4. Cards/Dialogs
